Our Mission & Values

Delhi Public School is committed to providing a holistic educational experience that fosters intellectual, social, and emotional growth. We believe in creating a supportive learning environment where every student can achieve their full potential.

Our core values of integrity, respect, and excellence guide us in shaping responsible global citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of the future. We follow the CBSE curriculum with a focus on practical application and critical thinking.

Academics

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Our primary curriculum focuses on foundational skills, encouraging curiosity and a love for learning through engaging, activity-based methods.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

We provide a structured environment to build on concepts, introduce new subjects, and develop analytical skills necessary for higher education.

Senior School (Grades 9-10)

Our senior curriculum prepares students for the CBSE board examinations, offering a wide range of subjects to choose from and focusing on career readiness.
